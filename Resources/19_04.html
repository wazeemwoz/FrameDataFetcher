Legend
  • Props to LennethEX, Dammit, C_cube blog and ComedyBlissOption for their work on 3s hitboxes.
  • : click to display hitboxes.
  • Startup corresponds to the number of frames BEFORE an event (hit/block) is registered by the game. Therefore, a 2 frames startup move will actually hit on the 3rd frame.
  • A 0 frame move hits the really next frame after the move is committed or right after the superfreeze for Super Arts. Therefore, you must strictly be on guard on this really next frame to block it.
  • All data, including hitboxes, extracted from CPS3 emulator (FBA-RR), except for column 'Parry' still based on old karathrow.com data.
  • 'Cancel' column shows cancel possibilities of the move, but mixes whiff/hit/block/cr.hit properties. Check hitboxes to get details.
  • Some whiffed moves may have some 'Cancel' properties even though it'll actually not work. Most obvious are shoryukens.
  • Damages measured against Gouki (lowest life in the game).
  • Bar Gain Att : gain of super bar for a move done by the attacker (whiff/hit/block).
  • Bar Gain Opp : gain of super bar for a move hitting opponent (hit/block).
  • 'Hit' column is the sum of the active frames of the move. It's kind of useless for multihits moves, so check hitboxes to get a more useful view.
